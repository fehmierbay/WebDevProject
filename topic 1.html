Access Control

  • Objective: To regulate access to sensitive data and systems within Poetry Foundation.
  • Implementation of the Principle of Least Privilege to grant access only as necessary for job roles.
  • Clear delineation of access rights based on employee responsibilities.
  • Use a sign-in/sign-out system or visitor logs to monitor who comes and goes.
  • Train employees on the importance of controlling access and on how to properly check identification and grant access.
  • Regularly review access permissions and revoke access for employees who no longer require it.

Authentication

  • Objective: To verify the identity of users accessing Poetry Foundation's systems and resources.
  • Use strong passwords for all computer systems and change them regularly.
  • Encrypt sensitive information to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Guidelines for creating and managing secure passwords, including periodic changes.
  • Train employees on the importance of protecting sensitive information and on the proper handling of data.
  • Store sensitive information, such as customer data, financial records in a secure manner.
  • Implementation of multi-factor authentication for enhanced security.
